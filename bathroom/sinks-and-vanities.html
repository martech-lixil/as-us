Bath Sinks + Vanities

American Standard offers a wide variety of bathroom sinks in different configurations - each offering the latest innovations and technology. Available as stand alone pedestals, above counter vessel sinks, and undermounted you can pair your sink with one of our bathroom sink vanities to elevate the look of your bathroom. Expand your storage options with a vanity that offers shelving or drawers to keep your bathroom looking neat and tidy. 

Bathroom Sink Basics

Your sink should not only enhance the visual appeal of your bathroom but also meet the practical needs of its users. Balancing style with functionality is key to choosing the right sink for your space.

Undermount

An under-mount sink sits under the counter with its edge below the level of the countertop creating a seamless counter that makes it easier to clean while hiding from view.  Shop All

Drop-In

A drop-in sink is placed into an opening with the rim creating a lip that forms a barrier between your sink and your counter making them an ideal choice for any bathroom refresh or remodel.
Shop All

Vessel

A basin-like bowl that is often used as a decorative and contemporary alternative to traditional undermount or drop-in sinks which makes it a popular choice for powder rooms. Shop All

Pedestal

Their sleek and clean design make them great for smaller spaces, just keep in mind you might need to supplement with additional storage solutions. Shop All
